WHO WE ARE


At Chaparral, we are passionate about providing exceptional quality and craftsmanship in designing and manufacturing a full line of interior closet doors and mirror products. With over 20+ years of industry experience, we have established ourselves as a leading provider of quality, custom-made interior doors and mirror products for residential, multi-family, and mixed-use markets.

Our team at Chaparral combines precision and attention to detail to create interior doors and mirror products that are visually stunning but also durable and functional. We understand that doors play a significant role in enhancing the aesthetics of any space, and we take pride in delivering solutions that exceed our customers’ expectations.

Whether a traditional mirror door, a stylish contemporary sliding closet door, or a beautiful, modern framed wall mirror, we offer a wide range of customizable options to suit various architectural styles and design preferences. From premium wood to high-grade aluminum and custom glass, we meticulously select the finest materials to ensure our products meet the highest quality standard.

As a customer-centric company, we value customer satisfaction and strive to build long-lasting customer relationships. Our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship, reliability, and customer service sets us apart in the industry.

Chaparral is owned by Patrick Industries, Inc., a publicly traded company (NASDAQ: PATK). Chaparral is a domestic company that manufactures components and builds products for industrial markets throughout the United States.
