Contemporary Sliding
Closet Doors


A popular choice for modern interior, our Contemporary Sliding Closet Doors offer a sleek and minimalist look while optimizing space for any interior. One defining feature of our collection is their smooth and effortless sliding mechanism. This smooth functionality ensures easy access to the contents of the closet while adding a touch of elegance to the room.

The Contemporary Sliding Closet Doors feature wide wood frames. Glass options from mirror to opaque white laminated glass, and back-painted polar white, compliment these beautiful wood frames. Decor rails are added customization options that come with the collection.
