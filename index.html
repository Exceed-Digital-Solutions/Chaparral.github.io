ABOUT US


At Chaparral, we are passionate about providing exceptional quality and craftsmanship in the design and manufacture of a full line of interior closet doors and mirror products. With over 20+ years of industry experience, we have established ourselves as a leading provider of quality, custom-made interior doors and mirror products for residential, multi-family and mixed-use markets.

Our team at Chaparral combines precision and attention to detail to create interior doors and mirror products that are not only visually stunning but also durable and functional. We understand that doors play a significant role in enhancing the aesthetics of any space, and we take pride in delivering solutions that exceed our customers’ expectations.

Whether it is a traditional mirror door, a stylish contemporary sliding closet door, or a beautiful, modern framed wall mirror, we offer a wide range of customizable options to suit various architectural styles and design preference. From premium wood to high-grade aluminum and custom glass, we meticulously select the finest materials to ensure our products meet the highest standard of quality.

As a customer-centric company, we value customer satisfaction and strive to build long-lasting relationships with our customers. Our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship, reliability and exceptional customer service sets us apart in the industry.

With our manufacturing facility located in Ontario, California, Chaparral joins the list of domestic manufacturers who proudly claim that we are Made in the USA.

Chaparral is owned by Patrick Industries, Inc., a publicly traded company (NASDAQ:PATK). Chaparral is a domestic company that engages in the manufacture of component products and of building products for industrial markets throughout the United States.
